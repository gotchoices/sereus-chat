# Editing Messages – Fixing a Small Mistake

Based on: `design/stories/editing-messages.md`

Persona: Alice (active participant)  
Preconditions: locale=en; Alice can edit her own messages

## 1) Notice a typo
Alice sees a small mistake in her last message and decides to fix it.
