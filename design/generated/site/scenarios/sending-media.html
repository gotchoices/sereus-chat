Sending Media – Sharing a Photo

Based on: design/stories/sending-media.md

Persona: Alex (active participant)
Preconditions: locale=en

1) Open the strand

Alex opens the conversation where he wants to send a photo.

2) Choose media

He taps the “+” and picks Camera or Gallery. A preview appears above the composer (placeholder in this build).

3) Send

He taps Send to share. The image appears in the conversation and the composer clears.
