Editing Messages – Fixing a Small Mistake

Based on: design/stories/editing-messages.md

Persona: Alice (active participant)
Preconditions: locale=en; Alice can edit her own messages

1) Notice a typo

Alice sees a small mistake in her last message and decides to fix it.

2) Enter edit mode

She opens the message menu and chooses Edit. The composer shows her original text so she can make a quick change.

3) Save and continue

She taps the check mark to save. The message updates in place, and she continues the conversation.
