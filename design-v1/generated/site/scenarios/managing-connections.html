Managing Connections – Finding and Organizing People

Based on: design/stories/managing-connections.md

Persona: Alex (returning user)
Preconditions: locale=en

1) Home with recent hebras

Alex opens the app and sees his recent hebras sorted by activity. It’s easy to scan names and previews at a glance.

2) Search to find a person or strand

He types a name to narrow down results and quickly lands on the strand he needs.

3) Returning to home

Back on home, Alex spots newly active hebras without losing context.
